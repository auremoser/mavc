"Information Visualization"


A way of representing information and data via a visual agenda.

The study of visual representations of abstract data to reinforce human cognition.

A method for communitcating quantitative information with qualitative nuance.

Goal of Visualizations

Represent information

in the simplest way

with minimum visual complexity

depending on your particular constraints.

ie beware chart junk

Approach to Visualizations


  • Understand your audience
  • Understand your data and desired narrative
  • Define a visual style and register
  • Build + Test
